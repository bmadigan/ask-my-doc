# Company Handbook

Welcome to Acme Technologies. This handbook outlines our policies, benefits, and workplace guidelines.

## Our Mission

Acme Technologies is dedicated to building innovative software solutions that empower businesses worldwide. We believe in creating products that are simple, reliable, and delightful to use.

## Time Off Policy

### Paid Time Off (PTO)

All full-time employees receive the following PTO:
- **First year**: 15 days of PTO
- **Years 2-4**: 20 days of PTO
- **Years 5+**: 25 days of PTO

PTO can be used for vacation, personal days, or sick time. Please submit PTO requests at least two weeks in advance for planned time off.

### Holidays

The company observes the following paid holidays:
- New Year's Day
- Memorial Day
- Independence Day
- Labor Day
- Thanksgiving (Thursday and Friday)
- Christmas Eve and Christmas Day

### Sick Leave

If you're feeling unwell, please stay home. Notify your manager as soon as possible. Extended illness (more than 3 consecutive days) may require a doctor's note.

## Remote Work Guidelines

Acme Technologies supports flexible work arrangements. Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week with manager approval.

### Remote Work Requirements
- Reliable internet connection
- Dedicated workspace
- Available during core hours (10am - 3pm local time)
- Attend all scheduled meetings via video

### In-Office Days

Teams should coordinate at least 2 days per week when everyone is in the office for collaboration and team building.

## Benefits Overview

### Health Insurance
We offer comprehensive medical, dental, and vision coverage. The company covers 80% of premiums for employees and 50% for dependents.

### Retirement
401(k) plan with 4% company match after 90 days of employment.

### Professional Development
Annual budget of $1,500 per employee for conferences, courses, and certifications.

### Wellness
Monthly wellness stipend of $50 for gym memberships, fitness classes, or mental health apps.

## Code of Conduct

We maintain a respectful, inclusive workplace. All employees are expected to:
- Treat colleagues with respect and professionalism
- Communicate openly and honestly
- Maintain confidentiality of company and client information
- Report any concerns to HR or management
